About

I’m a data analyst with a passion for uncovering insights that tell meaningful stories. I believe that data and curiosity are the most powerful tools to understand the world and drive change. Most of my days are spent exploring datasets, visualizing patterns, and building models that answer real questions. I enjoy the process of transforming raw, unstructured data into something clear and actionable. My core skills include data cleaning, visualization, storytelling, and machine learning. I’m deeply interested in finding what influences behavior why customers make certain choices, what patterns lie beneath the numbers, and how data can shape smarter decisions. Whn I’m not deep into Python or Power BI dashboards, I like to share what I learn. I write tutorials, simplify technical concepts, and explore ways to make data science approachable for everyone. I enjoy taking part in challenges on Kaggle, HackerRank, and other communities that encourage creative problem solving.

I believe in continuous learning, and I’m always looking for ways to combine technical skills with storytelling and strategy. Outside of work, I spend time experimenting with data projects that reflect everyday problems analyzing trends, visualizing topics that interest me, and sharing insights online. Through my work, I hope to show how data can do more than describe the world it can help improve it.
